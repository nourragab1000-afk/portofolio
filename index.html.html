Sustainable Scaling to EGP 233K

The Problem

The brand hit a ceiling — every time budget increased, CPA would explode and profitability would collapse. The account had audience overlap and bid competition destroying efficiency from within.

The Strategy
Account Restructure Lookalike Scaling Funnel Optimization Budget Pacing

Audited and rebuilt the entire account. Eliminated audience overlap with exclusions. Built Lookalike audiences from purchaser lists. Used a gradual 20% budget increase method to scale safely without shocking the algorithm.
